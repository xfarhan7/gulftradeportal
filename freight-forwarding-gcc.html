Frequently asked questions

How do I get a freight quote to the GCC?
Use the freight quote tool on Gulf Trade Portal. Enter your origin, GCC destination, freight mode, cargo type, and volume. Your request is sent to up to three verified logistics partners who respond with competing quotes — free and with no obligation.
Which GCC ports do you ship to?
Our logistics partners ship to all major Gulf ports including Jebel Ali (UAE), Jeddah and Dammam (Saudi Arabia), Hamad Port (Qatar), Sohar and Salalah (Oman), Shuwaikh (Kuwait), and Bahrain.
Do forwarders handle customs and VAT?
Yes. Verified partners offer full customs clearance, documentation, HS coding, VAT handling (5% UAE, 15% KSA), and duty calculation, in addition to freight. Many offer complete door-to-door service.
Is the freight quote service free?
Yes. Requesting freight quotes through Gulf Trade Portal is completely free with no obligation. Logistics partners compete for your business, which helps you get the best rate.
