Frequently asked questions

How do I find suppliers to the UAE?
Register free on Gulf Trade Portal and post an import request describing what you need. Verified suppliers from Europe, the US, and Asia respond directly. You can also browse the directory and filter by sector and region.
Can I re-export from the UAE through the platform?
Yes. Many UAE members use Gulf Trade Portal for re-export trade through Dubai free zones and Jebel Ali. Our logistics partners support bonded warehousing and re-export to the wider GCC and beyond.
Is there a commission on UAE deals?
No. Gulf Trade Portal charges no commission. It is a neutral matchmaking platform where UAE buyers and international suppliers connect and deal directly. Free to join, with optional paid plans.
Can I get shipping to Jebel Ali and Dubai?
Yes. Our logistics partners provide freight quotes, customs clearance, VAT handling, and door-to-door delivery to Jebel Ali, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and across the Emirates.
